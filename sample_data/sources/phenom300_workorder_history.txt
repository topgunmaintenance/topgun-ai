Work Order WO-2026-0418 — TOGA pushbutton intermittent
Aircraft: Phenom 300, Tail N742JP
Reported by: Captain on 2026-03-29
ATA 22

Discrepancy:
"TOGA pushbutton on captain side-stick intermittently fails to engage
TOGA mode in cool morning conditions. Function returns once the
cockpit warms up. No fault message on the FGC at any time."

Inspection:
Performed FIM 22-10-00 troubleshooting flow. The side-stick TOGA
discrete did not transition during the cold check at the captain
position; both side discretes worked normally at room temperature.

Corrective action:
Replaced the captain side-stick TOGA pushbutton micro-switch with
the supersession part listed in SB-22-04 per AMM 22-10-22. Performed
post-installation functional test on both side-sticks at room
temperature and on cold soak; all transitions normal. Updated the
aircraft logbook entry referencing SB-22-04 compliance.

Notes:
Two prior work orders on N742JP referenced the same symptom in
2025-11 and 2026-01 but were closed by re-seating the side-stick
connector P22-S04. SB-22-04 supersession appears to have resolved
the recurrence.
