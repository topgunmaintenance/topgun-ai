WDM 22-10-04 — TOGA Pushbutton Wiring Diagram
Embraer Phenom 300 — Wiring Diagram Manual, Rev 9
Aircraft: Phenom 300
ATA 22 Auto Flight

This sheet shows the wiring run between the captain side-stick TOGA
pushbutton and the Flight Guidance Computer discrete input on
connector P22-A12.

Connector and pin reference:
- Side-stick TOGA pushbutton harness end: connector P22-S04, pins 1
  and 2 (signal and return).
- Flight Guidance Computer end: connector P22-A12, pin 14 (TOGA
  discrete input) and pin 15 (signal return).
- Wire numbers: W22-1014 (signal) and W22-1015 (return). Both routed
  through the side console wire bundle to the avionics rack.

Continuity test points:
- TP-22-A: at the side-stick connector P22-S04 pin 1.
- TP-22-B: at the FGC connector P22-A12 pin 14.
- TP-22-C: at the FGC connector P22-A12 pin 15.

A continuity check between TP-22-A and TP-22-B with the connectors
mated should read less than 1 ohm. A failure indicates a chafed or
broken wire in the side console bundle — replace per AMM 92-10-04.
