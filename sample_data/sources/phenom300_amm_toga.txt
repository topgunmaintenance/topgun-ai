AMM 22-10-22 — TOGA Pushbutton Removal and Installation
Embraer Phenom 300 — Aircraft Maintenance Manual, Rev 32
Aircraft: Phenom 300
ATA 22 Auto Flight

This procedure covers removal and installation of the TOGA pushbutton
assembly on the side-stick controller.

Tools and materials:
- Standard avionics tool kit
- Calibrated torque driver (in-lb range)
- Lockwire per the materials list
- Anti-static wrist strap

Removal:
1. Apply external electrical power and verify both side-stick TOGA
   discretes are LOW on the FGC discrete input page, then remove
   electrical power.
2. Open and lock open the side console access panel per AMM 25-10-08.
3. Disconnect the TOGA pushbutton harness at connector P22-A12.
4. Remove the four retaining screws securing the pushbutton to the
   side-stick housing and lift the pushbutton away.

Installation:
1. Inspect the new pushbutton for damage and confirm part applicability.
2. Position the pushbutton in the side-stick housing and install the
   four retaining screws. Torque per the table in AMM 22-10-22 fig 3.
3. Reconnect the harness at connector P22-A12 and verify the connector
   latch is fully engaged.
4. Close the side console access panel.
5. Apply external electrical power and perform the functional test:
   press the TOGA pushbutton and confirm the FGC discrete transitions
   LOW->HIGH and that the autoflight system displays "TOGA" on the PFD.

Reference SB-22-04 for the superseded micro-switch part number.
