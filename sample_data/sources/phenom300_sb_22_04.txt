SB-22-04 — Service Bulletin: TOGA Pushbutton Micro-Switch Supersession
Embraer Phenom 300 — Service Bulletin SB-22-04, Issue 2
Aircraft: Phenom 300
ATA 22 Auto Flight

Subject: Replacement of the original side-stick TOGA pushbutton
micro-switch with the improved supersession part. The original
micro-switch has been associated with intermittent TOGA-not-engaged
events at intermediate ambient temperatures.

Applicability: Phenom 300 aircraft serial numbers 50500001 through
50500842 that have not already received the supersession.

Compliance: Recommended at the next scheduled inspection or upon any
report of intermittent TOGA mode failure.

Action: Remove and replace the affected TOGA pushbutton micro-switch
per AMM 22-10-22 using the supersession part listed in the latest
revision of the Phenom 300 IPC for ATA 22-10. Reference WDM 22-10-04
for connector and pin locations during the post-installation
functional check.

Note: This bulletin does not address chafing or wiring failures
between the side-stick and the FGC. Use FIM 22-10-00 for full fault
isolation.
